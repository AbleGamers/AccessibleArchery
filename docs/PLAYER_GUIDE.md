# 🏹 Accessible Archery — How to Play

**Play with whatever works for you.** Press a number key to pick your controls:

| Press | You play with | How |
|---|---|---|
| **1** | Keyboard | **Arrows / WASD** aim · hold **Space** to draw · release to fire |
| **2** | Gamepad / Adaptive controller | **Left stick** aims · hold **A** to draw · release to fire |
| **3** | One switch (or any single button) | Tap once to lock left–right · tap again to lock up–down · the bow draws and fires itself |
| **4** | Eye tracking (or just the mouse) | Look where you want to aim · hold your gaze still to draw · fires at full draw |
| **5** | Your voice | Say **left / right / up / down** to aim · **draw** · **loose** to fire · **center** to reset |

## The one rule of the bow

**Every shot needs a full draw.** Hold the draw until you feel/hear/see the
*snap* — the flash on screen, the tone reaching its peak, the thump in the
controller — then release. Let go early and the shot safely cancels.

## Scoring

- Rings score **10** (bullseye), **5**, or **1**.
- A match is sets of **3 arrows** against the CPU — win a set for 2 points,
  first to **6 set points** wins. Tied match? Sudden-death shootout, one arrow.
- Watch the **wind** at the top of the screen — it pushes your arrow!

## Put your name up in lights

Press **L** to bank your score to the **booth leaderboard** — it shows your
name AND how you played (🎙 voice, 🕹 switch, 👁 eye, ⌨ keyboard, 🎮 gamepad)
on the big screen behind you.

## Make it yours — press Esc

Everything is adjustable, no judgment:

- **Aim assist** — the bullseye gently attracts your arrow
- **Bigger targets** · **slower aim** · **more time to draw**
- **Audio aiming** — pings get faster and higher as you near the bullseye
  (the whole game is playable with your eyes closed)
- **Captions** for every sound · **rumble** for every cue
- **V** flips the camera if you'd rather shoot from the other side

## Playing without sight?

Turn up the volume. The pings pan toward the target and speed up as your aim
closes in — dead center is a rapid high trill. Draw, wait for the peak tone,
release. The crowd will tell you the rest. 🏟
